About Sire Bagh

Sire Bagh is a unique farm dining restaurant that blends the history of a noble estate with the simplicity and freshness of a farm-to-table dining experience. Set on a peaceful piece of farmland owned by an old noble family of Jodhpur, the beautiful estate is surrounded by fields, gardens, and orchards which offer a relaxing and rustic atmosphere providing an escape from the hustle and bustle of modern life.

Advocacy

Sustainability

The restaurant emphasizes on sustainability, using organic farming methods and minimizing waste. Our family takes pride in maintaining the land and ensuring that its resources are responsibly managed for future generations and everyone is committed to sustainable practices.

Empowerment

At Sire Bagh we believe in supporting our local community in every way possible. We emphasize on giving back to our people as much as we take from them and we do so by sourcing most of our ingredients and workforce from around the area. Sire Jawai

Jawai is a stunning terrain dominated by sheer granite outcrops interspersed with scrub forests and farmland. The diverse ecosystem and the granite hills provide an excellent environment for the wildlife, particularly leopards, which are abundant in this region as well as the sloth bears, crocodiles and numerous migratory birds.

Eco Friendly

Sire Jawai is a boutique resort with four private villas, which are being built keeping in mind the ecology and cultural sensitivities of the area with minimum impact to the surrounding flora and fauna.
