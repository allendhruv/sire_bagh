Feature Menu

At Sire Bagh the guests get an opportunity to relish some of our generations old household recipes which we have carefully curated into our present-day Rajasthani menu. The menu at Sire Bagh is seasonal, highlighting fresh ingredients grown on the estate or sourced from nearby farms and small grocers. Whether it's freshly picked vegetables, free-range meats, or homemade bread, our food is prepared with care and attention while staying rooted to Rajasthani tradition.
