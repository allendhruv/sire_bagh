Experience

Sire Bagh offers more than just great food – it gives guests a chance to connect and engage with the land, its history, and its vibrant culture.

Visitors can stroll through the estate’s gardens while enjoying some live folk music, explore the farm, learn about our sustainable farming practices, and interact with our in-house Chef and hosts. Our guests also get to indulge in a live cooking demonstration where they get to pick and forage ingredients from the farm, providing them with a hands-on experience.
